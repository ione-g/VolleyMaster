# Volley Master
An Android app designed to enhance volleyball training for players of all levels.

## Overview
Volley Master is a comprehensive tool for volleyball enthusiasts seeking to improve their skills and performance. The app offers personalized training programs, interactive drills, and progress tracking to help you become a better player.

## Features
- **Personalized Training Plans:** Create custom workouts based on your skill level and goals.
- **Skill-specific Drills:** Practice serving, passing, setting, spiking, blocking, and defense with targeted exercises.
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitor your improvements over time with detailed statistics.
- **Interective Tutorials:** Learn techniques from personal coach through instructional animation.

## Getting Started
1. **Profile Setup:** Enter your basic information and skill level.
2. **Choose a Training Program:** Select from preset plans, customize your own or we make personal plan for you.
3. **Start Training:** Follow daily exercises and drills.
4. **Track Your Progress:** Use the app's analytics to see your improvement.
